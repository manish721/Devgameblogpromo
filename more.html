03 — Publishing

Content Publishing Guidelines

01

Alignment with DGBP Pillars

  • All content must reflect Integrity, Authenticity, and Discipline
  • No clickbait, exaggerated claims, or misleading titles
  • Brand voice: direct, grounded, builder-focused
  • Founder approval required for statements on behalf of DGBP
02

Submission & Review Process

  • Submit drafts via the DGBP Board or official form
  • Core Team reviews within 48–72 hours
  • Two-round feedback before final approval
  • Published only after admin sign-off
03

AI-Generated Content

  • Must be disclosed in the post footer or metadata
  • Requires human review before publishing
  • Cannot represent member testimonials or case studies
  • AI tools must be listed in post credits
04

Prohibited Content

  • No unsolicited promotions or paid placements
  • No political, religious, or divisive opinions
  • No plagiarism or unattributed sourcing
  • No content targeting or disparaging individuals
05

Lab Updates & Reports

  • Each lab submits a monthly update by the 1st
  • Updates must include progress, blockers, next steps
  • Lab Leads are responsible for accuracy
  • All reports stored in Official Docs section
06

Archiving & Versioning

  • All published content is versioned and dated
  • Edits to live content require re-approval
  • Archived posts remain accessible but marked inactive
  • Deletion requires Founder-level authorization
04 — Conduct

Platform Rules & Standards

  • Respect All Members

    Every person in the DGBP ecosystem — learner, creator, or collaborator — deserves respectful, professional treatment. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind are not tolerated.

    Zero Tolerance

  • No Unsanctioned Promotion

    Self-promotion, affiliate links, or external brand plugs are not permitted in community spaces, labs, or content — unless explicitly approved by the Core Team.

    Requires Approval

  • Confidentiality of Internal Documents

    Internal roadmaps, hiring frameworks, financial details, and unpublished lab reports are confidential. Sharing outside DGBP without consent constitutes a serious violation.

    Strict Enforcement

  • Accurate Representation

    Members may not misrepresent their role, credentials, or association with DGBP in any external communication, social media, or professional profile.

    Monitor & Report

  • Discord & Community Standards

    All activity in the official Discord (and any affiliated channels) must follow the DGBP Community Code. Violations are reviewed by the Core Team and may result in removal.

    Community Moderated

  • Intellectual Property

    All work created within DGBP Labs, under mentorship, or as part of a course — unless otherwise agreed in writing — remains the intellectual property of the creating member. DGBP retains showcase rights.

    Policy Reference

  • Violation Escalation Process

    Minor violations receive a written warning. Repeated or severe violations are escalated to the Core Team for review. Serious misconduct may result in immediate removal with no appeal.

    Strictly Enforced
